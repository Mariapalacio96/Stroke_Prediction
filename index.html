Stroke Patient Dashboard

According to the CDC's Stroke Facts, nearly 800,000 strokes occur in the United States on an annual basis. Of those, there is approximately a 15% 30-day mortality rate. Even for people who survive, many can be left with either very long roads to recovery or ultimately serious long-term disability. By helping to uncover what factors are more likely to lead to strokes, we ultimately hope to help people avoid them in the first place.
